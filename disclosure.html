Amazon Associates Program

NeatNest Home is a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for sites to earn advertising fees by advertising and linking to Amazon.com.

Our Amazon affiliate tag is neatnest00-20. When you click a link to Amazon from this website and make a purchase (of any product, not necessarily the one we linked to), we receive a small commission. This commission comes from Amazon's revenue — it does not increase the price you pay.

How Affiliate Links Work

Affiliate links are special URLs that contain a tracking code. When you click one of our Amazon links:

  • Amazon records that you arrived through NeatNest Home.
  • If you make a qualifying purchase within 24 hours, we receive a percentage of the sale price.
  • Your purchase price is the same as if you had navigated to Amazon directly.

Amazon affiliate commissions typically range from 1% to 10% depending on the product category. They help us keep this site free and ad-light.

We maintain complete editorial independence. This means:

  • If we don't like a product, we say so — or we don't mention it at all.
  • Our ratings and opinions are our own, based on personal testing or extensive research.
