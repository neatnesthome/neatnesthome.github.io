Expert Advice

3 Kitchen Organization Tips

01

Group by task, not by type

Keep everything you need for morning coffee in one zone, baking supplies in another. You grab what you need without scanning every shelf.

02

Decant the staples you use daily

Flour, sugar, oats, and pasta look cohesive and stay fresher in airtight canisters. Everything else can stay in the original packaging in a labeled bin.

03

Front-face everything after each use

Takes two seconds — turn every bottle and jar label-forward when you put it back. The cabinet looks instantly more organized without any extra effort.
