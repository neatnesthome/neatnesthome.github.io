Pro Tips

3 Bathroom Organization Rules That Stick

Toss expired products before organizing

Before buying a single organizer, do a ruthless purge of expired medications, dried-up makeup, and products you haven't used in six months. Most bathroom clutter isn't disorganized — it's just stuff you don't need anymore.

Keep daily items at eye level

Products you use every day belong on the counter or at the front of your most accessible drawer. Reserve under-sink storage for backup products and items you use weekly or less.

Use vertical wall space aggressively

Bathrooms have limited floor and counter space but often unused wall space. A simple wall-mounted shelf or a few Command hooks can double your storage without touching the floor plan.
