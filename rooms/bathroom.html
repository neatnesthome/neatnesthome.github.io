Expert Advice

3 Bathroom Organization Tips

01

Clear the counter completely

Take everything off your bathroom counter. Only put back what you use every single day. Everything else gets a home inside a cabinet or drawer. The counter will feel twice as large.

02

Remove empties immediately

Keep a small wastebasket in the shower. The moment a bottle is empty, it goes in. Most bathroom clutter is just expired or empty products taking up prime real estate.

03

Use the door and walls

Over-door organizers, wall-mounted holders, and adhesive hooks turn wasted vertical space into useful storage. In a small bathroom, the walls are your biggest organizational asset.
