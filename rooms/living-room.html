Expert Advice

3 Living Room Organization Tips

01

Use a tray to define "surface zones"

A decorative tray on the coffee table instantly defines what belongs there and what doesn't. Items in the tray look intentional. Items outside the tray look like clutter. Same stuff — very different result.

02

Every horizontal surface needs a purpose

Shelves, side tables, and windowsills that are "just for stuff" become dumping grounds. Give each surface one intentional purpose — display, books, plants, or storage — and stick to it.

03

Store things where you actually use them

If you always watch TV from the sofa, keep remotes, chargers, and blankets within arm's reach. Organization only works when the storage location matches actual behavior.
