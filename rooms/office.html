Expert Advice

3 Home Office Organization Tips

01

Clear the desk to just three things

Your monitor, your keyboard, and one thing you're currently working on. Everything else has a drawer, shelf, or box. Visual clutter competes directly with focus.

02

Tame cables before anything else

Cable chaos makes any desk look disorganized no matter how tidy everything else is. A cable management box under the desk costs $20 and removes 80% of visual noise instantly.

03

Give every supply a category, not a pile

Drawer organizers with labeled sections mean you always know exactly where the stapler, tape, and sticky notes live. Ten minutes to set up saves five minutes every single day.
