Pro Tips

3 Office Organization Rules for Deep Focus

Clear desk at the end of every workday

Spend two minutes at the end of each day returning everything to its place. You'll start the next morning with a clear surface and a clear head — the mental reset alone is worth the habit.

Tame cables before organizing anything else

Visible cable chaos is one of the biggest sources of desk clutter stress. Solve it once with a cable box or cable clips and your entire setup will feel more intentional, even before you add a single organizer.

Only keep active projects on your desk

Your desk surface is prime real estate for active work — not storage. Anything you haven't touched in a week belongs in a drawer, a file, or out of the room entirely.
