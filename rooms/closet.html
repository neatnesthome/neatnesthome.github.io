Expert Advice

3 Closet Organization Tips

01

Switch to slim velvet hangers first

Before buying any system or adding any storage, switch every hanger to velvet. You'll instantly gain 30% more space — often enough that you don't need anything else.

02

Organize by outfit, not by type

Instead of grouping all shirts together, group outfits by occasion — work, casual, formal. Getting dressed becomes effortless and you'll see exactly what you own.

03

Use every inch of vertical space

Most closets have at least one shelf with wasted vertical space above it. Add a second shelf, an over-door organizer, or a hanging accessory organizer to recover that space.
