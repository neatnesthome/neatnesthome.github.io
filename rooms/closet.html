Pro Tips

3 Closet Habits That Keep Everything in Order

Organize by outfit, not by color

Color-coding looks beautiful in photos but makes getting dressed harder. Group clothes by how you actually wear them — workwear together, casual together, occasion wear together — and mornings become faster.

Turn all hangers backward twice a year

Start each season with all hangers facing backward. When you wear something and put it back, face the hanger forward. After six months, anything still backward hasn't been worn — and probably won't be.

One-in-one-out, without exception

Every time a new item enters your closet, one item leaves. This single rule prevents closet creep and means you never need a "big clear-out" again.
