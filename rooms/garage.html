Expert Advice

3 Garage Organization Tips

01

Get everything off the floor first

Floor space is for cars and feet — everything else goes on walls, shelves, or the ceiling. If you can't park a car in your garage, floor clutter is the problem, not a lack of storage.

02

Zone by frequency of use

Daily items go at eye level and within easy reach. Monthly items go on high shelves or the back wall. Seasonal items go overhead. Distance from the door should equal rarity of use.

03

Label everything — without exception

Unlabeled bins become junk drawers within a month. Every bin, shelf, and hook should have a clear label. When everything has a labeled home, every member of the household can maintain the system.
