Pro Tips

3 Garage Organization Rules That Last

Get everything off the floor first

Floor clutter is the enemy of a functional garage. Before organizing shelves and bins, commit to getting 100% of items off the garage floor. Wall hooks, overhead racks, and shelving units pay for themselves in reclaimed floor space.

Zone by frequency of use

Items you reach for weekly go at chest height on accessible shelving. Seasonal items — holiday decorations, camping gear — belong in overhead storage or the back of deep shelves. Use zones ruthlessly and stick to them.

Label every bin before you fill it

Clear bins only solve half the problem — you can see the contents, but you can't read labels from across the garage. Label the front and side of every bin in large text so you can spot what you need without moving anything.
