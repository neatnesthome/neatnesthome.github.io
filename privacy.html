Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information NeatNest Home collects when you visit https://neatnesthome.github.io, how we use it, and what rights you have regarding your information.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

If you contact us via our contact page, we collect the information you voluntarily provide — typically your name, email address, and the content of your message. We use this only to respond to your inquiry.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we may collect certain information automatically when you visit our site, including:

  • Your IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages you visit and time spent on each page
  • Referring URLs (how you found us)
  • Device type (desktop, mobile, tablet)

This data is collected in aggregate to help us understand which content is most helpful. It is not used to identify individual visitors.

2. Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. NeatNest Home may use cookies for:

  • Analytics: To understand traffic patterns and which pages are most popular.
  • Affiliate tracking: Amazon and other affiliate networks use cookies to track clicks and attribute commissions. These cookies are placed by Amazon when you click our affiliate links, not by NeatNest Home directly.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that some site features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

3. Amazon Affiliate Program & Third-Party Links

This site participates in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program. When you click an Amazon link on this site, Amazon may place cookies on your device to track your purchase. Amazon's privacy practices are governed by Amazon's Privacy Policy, not ours.

Our site may link to other third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to read their privacy policies.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to contact form submissions
  • Analyze site traffic to improve our content
  • Understand which products and topics are most helpful to our readers

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Data Retention

Contact form inquiries are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your message and are not stored in any external database.

Analytics data is retained in aggregate, anonymized form and not tied to individual users.

6. Children's Privacy

NeatNest Home is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • The right to access information we hold about you
  • The right to request deletion of your information
  • The right to opt out of certain data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information you share with us. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we'll update the "last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please use our contact page. We're happy to address any concerns.
